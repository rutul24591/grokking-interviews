# Audit Checklist

Use this checklist when auditing one article or a full sub-category.

## Structural checks

- Required sections exist
- Required sections are in the correct order
- Interview section is second-last
- References section is last
- No implementation-example sections remain
- No code blocks or `<pre>` tags remain

## Content checks

- The article is deep enough for senior-level preparation
- The article avoids repetitive paragraphs
- The article explains trade-offs and failure modes
- The article uses paragraph-heavy writing rather than bullet-heavy notes
- Interview questions are detailed and topic-specific

## Visual checks

- SVG count is at least 3 where the topic supports it
- SVGs are not placeholders
- SVGs support the article's teaching goals

## Reference checks

- References exist
- References feel credible and sufficiently technical

## Output shape

Use:

```text
Sub-category Name:

Article Name:
Rating:
Issues Found:
Suggestions:
```
