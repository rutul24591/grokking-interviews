This is a runnable “SLA Lab” demo for **availability SLAs**:

- Model a service’s availability from its dependencies (serial vs redundant).
- Compute expected monthly downtime and error budget minutes.
- Run a simple Monte Carlo simulation to illustrate why independence assumptions can be wrong.
- A Node agent exercises the calculator endpoint.

Interview mapping:
- How to decompose availability across tiers and dependencies.
- Why redundancy changes math (parallel composition).
- Why correlated failures break naive calculations.
